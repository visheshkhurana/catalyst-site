Why AI-first is different

AI-first companies aren't SaaS companies with a model bolted on.

They start smaller, move faster, and hit different walls — model access, compute costs, evals, distribution, and investors who don't understand why inference is a line item. A generic accelerator treats them like any other startup and hands them the same generic advice.

Catalyst is built for exactly one kind of company. We write the first check, put you in a small room of serious AI-first founders, and give you the credits, mentors, and customers that are specific to building with AI at the core.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What stage do you invest at?
Idea to early traction. Pre-seed and even pre-product are welcome — we back founders early and often.
Do I need a demo or revenue to apply?
No. We look for technical founders who can build fast and a real, unfair insight into the problem. A prototype helps but isn't required.
What are the terms?
We write the first check on a simple, standard SAFE — the same terms for every company in the batch, with no negotiation. We share the specifics when we make you an offer.
Is the program remote or in person?
Remote-first, with an in-person kickoff and Demo Day. You keep building where you are, and we come together for the moments that matter.
Do you only back AI companies?
Yes — AI-first only. It's the one thing we do, so the check, the credits, the mentors, and the network are all tuned for it.
When is the next batch?
Applications for Batch 01 are open and reviewed on a rolling basis. Apply any time — earlier is better.
