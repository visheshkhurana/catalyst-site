Why exceptional founders still lose 18 months

The bottleneck was never money. It was time.

Capital has been abundant for a decade. Yet the most capable builders alive still spend their first year and a half on work that has nothing to do with their product — incorporation, hiring, payroll, contracts, fundraising decks, back-office, dashboards. The idea waits while the plumbing gets built, badly, from scratch, for the thousandth time.

We think that's a waste of rare talent. So we built the plumbing once — and put an AI workforce behind it. A founder who joins Catalyst skips the drag and starts at the frontier.

The engine — Catalyst OS

Agents execute. Humans decide.

Catalyst OS orchestrates a workforce of specialized agents across the entire build line. Senior partners set direction and own every decision-gate; agents do the undifferentiated execution — so the marginal cost of starting a company falls toward zero, and every build trains the next.
